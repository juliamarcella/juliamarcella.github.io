Selected work

01
Menstrual Cycle & Productivity

How much does a menstrual cycle actually affect someone's ability to work — and can we measure it? Using a dataset from a period-tracking app, I analysed how cycle-related symptoms, biological factors, and lifestyle choices impact productivity, measured through hours of work lost, concentration, and mood scores. The analysis uncovers which symptoms hit hardest, whether lifestyle adjustments make a real difference, and builds a predictive model estimating how many work hours a person may lose. The Looker Studio dashboard makes these insights accessible — a practical tool for building more inclusive, wellbeing-aware workplaces.

Python Looker Studio Period Tracking Data Machine Learning
GitHub Presentation Dashboard
02
Women in the Olympics

120 years of Olympic data holds a story most people have never read. I wanted to ask: how much has actually changed for women in sport, and where does inequality still hide? The analysis traces women's participation by country, decade, and discipline — surfacing which nations lead, which lag, and how cultural and geopolitical factors shaped women's access to competition over time. Behind every medal count is a question of who was even allowed to show up.

Python SQL Looker Studio Historical Data
GitHub Dashboard
03
E-Commerce Customer Analytics

A business sitting on transaction data is sitting on unanswered questions: Who are our most valuable customers? What drives a second purchase? Where does the funnel break? I used customer segmentation and behavioural analysis to move beyond surface-level revenue numbers — identifying purchasing patterns, retention risks, and the moments that define customer loyalty. The Power BI dashboard translates that analysis into decisions a business can act on.
